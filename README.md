# TubeForge AI Studio — Releases

**The local‑first AI video studio for YouTube creators.**
Turn a one‑line idea into a finished, published YouTube video — script, voiceover, scenes, motion render, thumbnail and upload — all on your own machine, your GPU, your channel.

## What it does

100% local rendering on your GPU · **bring‑your‑own AI keys** (free options to start) · AI scripting, a local motion engine, AI image/voice/**video‑clip** generation, experiment tracking, monetization signals, and **direct YouTube publishing**. No render farm, no per‑minute billing, no desktop telemetry.
